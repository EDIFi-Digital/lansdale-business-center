Convenience

We have everything you need to work comfortably, from a personal mailbox and custom door plaque to conference room access and outdoor meeting space with Wi-Fi. Enjoy a private walking path for refreshing breaks and easily manage shipping with on-site UPS, FedEx, and USPS services!

Accessibility

Enjoy 24/7 access to your workspace with ample private off-street parking. Let us help you find a solution that meets your operational requirements and style. Whether you prefer working late or early, our flexible access lets you work your way, anytime that suits your schedule!

Services

Our services are designed to make your workday seamless! From front desk support to greet your visitors and handle packages, to a full-time on-site management team, we've got you covered. Available scanning, faxing, recycling, and snow removal and lawn care. We help you focus on what matters most, the success of your business!

Utilities

Stay powered and connected! Our spaces are equipped with dependable Borough of Lansdale electric, and high-speed internet, readily available to keep your work flowing without interruptions. Whether you're streaming, uploading, or powering through tasks, we've got you covered!

Offices

Our well appointed and furnished offices are designed to meet your unique needs, offering a variety of sizes and styles from which to choose. Each space comes with full front desk support, all utilities included, and access to shared amenities like a conference rooms and kitchenettes. It's everything you need to work comfortably and efficiently!

Shop/Studio/Flex Spaces

Functional and practical. No two spaces are alike. Ranging between 450 and 3,000 sq ft and a variety of ceiling heights. All spaces are equipped with separately metered Borough of Lansdale Electric Service. Certain spaces also offer Peco natural gas. A handful of spaces offer private sink and drain. Of course we will be looking at spaces with features important to your work such as ground level, dock height, or overhead door.
