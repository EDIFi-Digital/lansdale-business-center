Location, Location, Location!

Part of the Greater North Penn area and neighbored by Upper Gwynedd, as well as Upper and Lower Bucks Counties. Lansdale Business Center is in proximity to numerous important roadways and local agencies. Just minutes from the Lansdale interchange of the Pennsylvania Turnpike and the Northeast Extension (Interstate 476) connecting to the PA 63 Bypass, or Main Street, Lansdale.

Visitors will want to know that Lansdale is also served by SEPTA Regional Rail's Lansdale/Doylestown Line via Lansdale station with service to Center City Philadelphia and Doylestown, as well as three SEPTA Suburban Division bus routes: Route 94 connects the southern part of Lansdale with the Montgomery Mall and Chestnut Hill‍

Lansdale Business Center

Lansdale is the headquarters of the Pennsylvania Northeastern Railroad, a short-line freight railroad. The Pennsylvania Northeastern Railroad operates the Lansdale Yard in the borough and provides freight rail service out of Lansdale to several points in the northern suburbs of Philadelphia on SEPTA-owned lines. The railroad interchanges with CSX Transportation in Lansdale.
