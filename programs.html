Lansdale Mural Arts Program!

By the end of 2019, the fourth original mural was installed on another building facade by LMAP. The Lansdale Mural Arts Program's mission since 2013 has been to help make Lansdale more beautiful, One Huge Canvas at a Time! And no canvas yet shines brighter than the home of LMAP’s fourth piece at the VFW on Cannon Avenue & 3rd Streets in town.

Annual Christmas Tree Display

For 12 Seasons under the umbrella of Discoverlansdale.org, the Annual Christmas Tree Display program surprised and delighted visitors at numbers of great locations in town. And it always demands mentioning how grateful we were to all 8 Brave Building Owners/Managers for trusting a “Merry Band of Elves” to come into their homes for business, at least once!

The annual display countinues to spread cheer! Each Holiday Season highlights LMAP: The Lansdale Mural Arts Program & Discover Lansdale. The display also partners with many participating businesses and peer organizations in the area. A wholly volunteer effort, each year showcases these vital area businesses and non-profits in Lansdale.  Want to learn more? There is so much you can do to help in making Lansdale a more beautiful place to live, work, and play! Again, so many Thanks to our visitors and supporters – We could not have done it without you! See www.discoverlansdale.org for info on this program and many others to enjoy.

Pets R People Too!

Held on the Front Lawn of the Lansdale Business Center, this Special Event is offered as a FUN-draiser for the Pet Pantry at Manna on Main Street, Lansdale, and for LMAP, The Lansdale Mural Arts Program. Free to attend, with the hope that guests make monetary donations and/or contribute pet food, or both, to these two important efforts to and for Lansdale.
